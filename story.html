How We Met & The Proposal

A tale of Alaska, ambushes, and a suspiciously shiny rock.

How We Met

Cyni and Tyler met in Wrangell, Alaska after Cyni suffered some traumatic head injury that made her forget he was a stinky fisherman.

Tyler lost no time in convincing her to move in with him and later to move to the deserts of Arizona.

The Proposal

On Valentine’s Day our unsuspecting heroine was ambushed by Tyler again after he trapped her at the edge of a massive hole in the ground and blinded her with a shiny rock on a metal band.

She was so confused that she agreed to whatever he said… for once.
