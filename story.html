Our Story

How We Met & The Proposal

Two chapters: Alaska → Arizona… and one extremely suspicious Valentine’s plan.

How We Met

Cyni and Tyler met in Wrangell Alaska after Cyni suffered some traumatic head injury that made her forget he was a stinky fisherman. Tyler lost no time in convincing her to move in with him and later to move to the deserts of Arizona.

The Proposal

On Valentines day our unsuspecting heroin was ambushed by Tyler again after he trapped her at the edge of a massive hole in the ground and blinded her with a shiny rock on a metal band. She was so confused that she agreed to whatever he said. .. for once.

Looking Forward

Now having survived Alaska, Arizona and an ambush at the Grand Canyon, we are ready to celebrate with our favorite people- under the trees and stars. Possiably near another scenic drop-off.

Thank you for being part of our story — we’re so glad you’re here.
